The Event Format

THREE DAYS.
ONE CHAMPION.

Every session builds toward Sunday's showdown. Whether you're racing or watching, the Zim Grand Prix delivers an experience unlike anything Zimbabwe has seen before.

01
Day One · Friday · August 2026
ARRIVAL
  • Morning Welcome Function Official opening ceremony. Meet drivers, teams, and the ZGP community.
  • Afternoon Practice Sessions Learn the circuit. Dial in your kart. Find your rhythm before it counts.
  • Evening Qualifying — Time Trials One flying lap. Your fastest time locks in your starting position. Every tenth matters.
02
Day Two · Saturday · August 2026
BATTLE
  • All Day Heat Races Shorter, intense elimination races. Only the quickest drivers advance to Sunday's final.
  • Parallel Family Festival Food vendors, live entertainment, and a full festival atmosphere alongside the racing.
  • Evening Corporate Team Challenge Companies battle for the Brand of the Year title. Business rivalry. Track-style.
03
Day Three · Sunday · August 2026
GLORY
  • Afternoon Championship Final — 40 Laps The top drivers. The same karts. Pure skill. $3,000 to the winner.
  • Post-Race Podium Ceremony Champagne. Prize money. Signed posters. The moment you've been working toward.
  • 🎵 Night After Party — Live Music Local artists perform live. The circuit becomes a concert. HERO Pass, VIP & after party add-on holders only.
Sunday Night · August 2026 · After the Final

Professional Kart

Technical Support
